I've watched this miniseries as part of my exploration of American history films. It covers fifty years from the Boston Massacre through Adams and Jefferson's deaths on July 4, 1826. Paul Giamatti's portrayal of a pissed off and depressed Adams is wonderful, the production quality is beautiful, and much of the dialogue derives from actual historical correspondence. It's a dad's period piece — recommended for those interested in learning American history through slower-paced television.
