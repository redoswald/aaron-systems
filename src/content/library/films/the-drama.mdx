The movie is enormously entertaining. The moment I knew it was going to be good was when I watched the trailer and learned almost nothing about the plot. It was basically just: here are two famous actors. What happens if we do something with them?

Pattinson and Zendaya pull it off. They're both pretty great.

Honestly, most of the criticism I've seen seems to come down to people not really getting the movie, which is kind of a them problem. They may need to think a little harder about certain parts of life if they want to understand what it's doing.

I can't really say more without getting spoilery, and I'm fine leaving it there.
