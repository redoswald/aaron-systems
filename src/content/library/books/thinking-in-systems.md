The best introduction to systems thinking I've found. Meadows explains feedback loops, stocks and flows, and leverage points with clarity and memorable examples.

Her list of "leverage points" - places to intervene in a system - is something I return to regularly. The most powerful leverage point? The mindset or paradigm out of which the system arises.
