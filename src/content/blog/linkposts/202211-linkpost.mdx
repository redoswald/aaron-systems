## Philosophy & Human Nature

**Emotional Liberation Through Transition** ([theguardian.com](https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2020/jul/20/i-forgot-how-to-cry-as-a-man-hrt-gave-me-a-range-of-emotions-i-never-thought-possible))
