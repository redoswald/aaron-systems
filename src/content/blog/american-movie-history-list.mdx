---

#### *Pre Revolution*

1. **The Last of the Mohicans** — Colonial America, French & Indian War

#### *Revolution*

2. **Hamilton** — Founding era politics, the early republic
3. **John Adams HBO miniseries** - 7 hours of serious treatment

#### *Ante-bellum*

4. **12 Years a Slave** — Antebellum slavery
5. **Amistad** (1997) - Black freedom
6. **The Good Lord Bird -** John Brown miniseries

#### *Civil War*

7. **Glory** — Civil War, Black soldiers in the Union Army
8. **Dances with Wolves** — Plains frontier and U.S. expansion
9. **Lincoln** — Civil War politics and the 13th Amendment

#### *Reconstruction + Wild West*

10. **Birth of a Nation/Gone with the Wind** – pick your poison
11. **Little Big Man** — 19th-century West, mythmaking and violence
12. **My Darling Clementine** — Tombstone-era frontier legend and law
13. **Shane** — Settlers, homesteads, and the "closing" frontier
14. **Unforgiven** — A darker late-frontier coda

#### *Gilded Age*

15. **There Will Be Blood** — Oil boom, capitalism, modern America forming
16. **Matewan** — Coal wars and U.S. labor history
17. **Killers of the Flower Moon** — 1920s Osage murders and extraction

#### *World War 1*

18. **Sergeant York** — biography of most decorated WW1 American
19. **The Lost Battalion** — Battalion of soldiers surrounded by Germans

#### *Great Depression*

20. **The Grapes of Wrath** — Great Depression and displacement
21. **Sullivan's Travels** — Depression-era America through Hollywood's mirror
22. **Bonnie and Clyde** — Depression crime, media, and modern celebrity
23. **To Kill a Mockingbird** — Jim Crow South and American justice
24. **Mr. Smith Goes to Washington**

#### *World War 2*

25. **Oppenheimer** — WWII science, politics, and the bomb
26. **Saving Private Ryan** — WWII ground-level experience
27. **Patton** — WWII command, ego, and strategy
28. **The Best Years of Our Lives** — WWII veterans coming home

#### *Post War Era*

29. **God Father Part I** — tension between old-world codes and American capitalism
30. **God Father Part II** — Immigrant experience (flashbacks)
31. **On the Waterfront** — Postwar labor, corruption, conscience
32. **Good Night, and Good Luck** — McCarthyism and broadcast journalism
33. **Thirteen Days** — Cuban Missile Crisis decision-making

#### *Space Program*

34. **The Right Stuff** — Mercury program, Cold War competition
35. **Hidden Figures** — Civil rights era through NASA's engine room
36. **Apollo 11 (documentary)** - The Moon landing
37. **First Man** - movie about Neil Armstrong
38. **Apollo 13** — 1970s NASA, competence under stress

#### *Vietnam*

39. **Platoon** — Vietnam as lived experience
40. **The Fog of War** — Documentary, Cold War and Vietnam through McNamara

#### *Civil Rights +*

41. **In the Heat of the Night** — Civil rights era tensions and policing
42. **Selma** — Voting rights movement and state power
43. **Malcolm X** — Identity, ideology, and the 1960s
44. **Judas and the Black Messiah** — COINTELPRO, Black Panthers, state response
45. **Milk** - biography of Harvey Milk
46. **Chinatown** - Water wars and environmental conflict
47. **Norma Rae** - Women's Rights and Unionization

#### *End of Consensus*

48. **All the President's Men** — Watergate and institutions
49. **Frost/Nixon** — Power, narrative, and accountability after Watergate
50. **The Insider** — Big Tobacco, whistleblowing, media
51. **Wall Street** (1987) — Financialization, greed is good

#### *The War on Terror and Great Recession*

52. **Black Hawk Down** — The end of American internationalism
53. **United 93** — 9/11 in real time, collective action
54. **The Hurt Locker** — Iraq War, adrenaline, moral corrosion
55. **Zero Dark Thirty** — Post-9/11 intelligence world and the bin Laden hunt
56. **The Big Short** — 2008 crisis as modern American history
