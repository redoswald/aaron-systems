# Welcome

Hi, and welcome to my site.

This is my own little version of a minimalist digital garden. In the past, I tried to make it a digital maximalist—and as you can read here, that did not work out so well.

## What you’ll find here

The main thing on this site is a history of the different things I’ve written.

* My **Goodreads** profile and **Letterboxd** profile have been ported over, and they’re accessible for people to browse and read.
* I also have a list of my **projects**.

## Projects and notes

I’m often messing around with data tools—writing random things in Python that may not be useful for anyone but me, but that bring me a little bit of joy. I’m going to write about and/or record those projects here, especially the ones I’ve done in the past, so there’s a place they can live.

## Closing

I hope you enjoy what’s here.
